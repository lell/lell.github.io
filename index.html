Our lab studies statistical machine learning, with applications to health science and ecology. Our methods include Bayesian nonparametrics, deep learning, phylogenetics, and genome-wide association study. We have results in brain imaging genetics, COVID-19 host genetics, the scientific study of aging, and theoretical machine learning. The trainees in our lab co-organize VanML: Vancouver Machine Learning. VanML holds yearly hackathons, workshops, or conferences.

Media coverage: Washington Post, Vancouver Sun, Jerusalem Post, Wired.
